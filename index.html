About Ren Kaltenheuser

Karen "Ren" Kaltenheuser is an intuitive creative + aspiring developer who believes in asking the “big” questions and inviting others to a deeper sense of embodiment. They seek to accomplish this through pen markings, blocks of text, muted colors, and lines of code.
They are based in New York, NY and received their BA in Graphic Design + Sociology in 2012. Since then, they have done graduate work at different prestigious art universities; they currently attend Columbia University for a Software Engineering bootcamp.
Their work has brought them across five continents now and has allowed them to work with editorial publications, politicians, universities, non-profits, entrepreneurs, international DJ’s, and more..
